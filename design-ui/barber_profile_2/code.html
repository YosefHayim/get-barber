Booking Services

How do I book a barber to my home? expand_more
simply enter your address on the home screen, browse available barbers in your area, select your services, and choose a time slot. Once confirmed, the barber will arrive at your location fully equipped.
Can I choose a specific barber? expand_more
Yes! You can view individual barber profiles, check their portfolios, read reviews, and see their specific pricing before booking.
Do you offer same-day appointments? expand_more
Absolutely. Use the "Immediate Availability" filter to find barbers who can be at your door within the hour.

Payments

What payment methods are accepted? expand_more
We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Cash payments are not supported for safety and convenience reasons.
When am I charged? expand_more
A temporary hold is placed on your card when you book. The actual charge is processed only after the haircut is completed to your satisfaction.

Cancellations

What is the cancellation policy? expand_more
You can cancel for free up to 2 hours before your scheduled appointment. Cancellations made within 2 hours may incur a 50% fee to compensate the barber for their time.
Can I reschedule instead of cancelling? expand_more
Yes, rescheduling is free of charge if done at least 2 hours prior to the appointment. Go to 'My Bookings' and select 'Reschedule'.
